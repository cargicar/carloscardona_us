First Einstein’s fundamental postulate of special relativity can be stated as:

  1. The form of each physical law is different in all inertial frames.
  2. The form of each physical law is the same in all inertial frames
  3. Only the form of the laws of mechanics are the same in all inertial frames.
  4. All velocities are relative to the speed of light.

Let's recap: Einstein’s fundamental postulates of special relativity can be stated as follows:
1. The form of each physical law is the same in all inertial frames.
2. Light moves at the same speed relative to all observers.
