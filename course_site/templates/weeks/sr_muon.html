Mathematical Description
Notation: $t_0=$ Propert time and $\ell_0=$ Proper lenght.

Time Dilation: $$\Delta t=\gamma\, \Delta t_0 $$ Length Contraction $$\Delta \ell=\frac{\ell_0}{\gamma}\,.$$
