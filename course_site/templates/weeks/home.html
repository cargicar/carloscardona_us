Research

Theoretical Physics

The bulk of my research in theoretical physics falls under the larger domain of Quantum Field Theory. Specifically, my inquiries center around the examination of Conformal Field Theories and Scattering Amplitudes in the realm of high-energy physics.
A list of my research work can be found at my Google Scholar profile.

Deep Learning

I am also interested in understanding the theoretical foundations of Deep Learning. For that, I like to look for useful analogies between Neural Networks and physical systems. In particular, I am interested in the optimization problem in Deep Neural Networks as well as the explotation of symmetries to improve learning training and generalization.
