A little bit about my life and background

I was born and raised in the beautiful lands of Colombia. As most countries in the Americas, Colombia’s population is largely mixed, with all sort of races and ethnicities. In fact, one of the most distinct features of the country is it’s diversity across the board, not only on the DNA traces of its inhabitants, but also has one of the largest bio-diversities in the planet. It is raked as the second most biodiverse country in the whole world {see here } only after Brasil, which in fairness covers eight times more land than Colombia! { In squared kilometers}.
